NFL Draft to be held in Green Bay

The 2025 NFL draft is to be held at Lambeau Field, in Green Bay Wisconsin. This will be the first time Green Bay is hosting a major NFl event. Event attendance is expected to be about 250,000 over the three days. CEO Mark Murphy, the Packers President, has been waiting to host the draft at Lambeau for over nine years, and it is finally happening this year. Learn more about Green Bay and the NFl draft below.
