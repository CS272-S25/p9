Rory McIlory Wins the Masters

On Sunday March 13th, Rory McIlroy won the Masters. This marks the first win for McIlroy after his debut to the tournament back in 2009. With this victory McIlroy achieved a career grand slam by winning in all four major championships (The Masters, PGA Championship, U.S. Open, and The Open Championship).
